Avia

A web design page aimed at indie game developers looking to showcase their work and connect with other developers. The homepage uses a colorful, modern design to create a fun and approachable atmosphere. The product pages feature detailed information on different games, including screenshots, trailers, and developer bios. The site also includes a forum where developers can share tips and collaborate on projects.

Noir Games

A web design page that promotes and sells escape room experiences. The homepage uses a dynamic layout with interactive graphics to create a sense of excitement and mystery. The product pages feature photos and descriptions of different escape rooms, along with reviews from satisfied customers. The site also includes a booking system so users can easily reserve a room.

Insanity

A web design page focused on fantasy-themed board games and RPGs. The homepage uses rich, dark colors and ornate graphics to create a medieval atmosphere, while the product pages feature detailed descriptions and reviews from other users. The site also includes a blog section where writers share their thoughts on various aspects of fantasy gaming.

Modular Nature

A web design page that allows users to explore and learn about different gaming lifestiles. The homepage uses a rich, full color landscape design with high-quality images to showcase some of the most famous places. The product pages provide detailed information on different gaming experiences, along with interactive exhibits and digital resources. The site also includes a search function so users can easily find specific pieces or styles.

Pixel Portal

A web design page dedicated to showcasing and selling retro-style pixel art. The homepage features a colorful banner with a rotating selection of pixel art pieces, while the product pages use a clean, minimalist design to let the art shine. The site also includes a community section where users can share their own pixel art creations.

Steampunk Games

A web design page that caters to fans of Japanese anime and manga. The homepage uses bright colors and bold typography to create an energetic, youthful vibe. The product pages feature detailed information on various anime series, including synopses, episode lists, and character bios. The site also includes a community section where fans can connect with each other and discuss their favorite anime.
